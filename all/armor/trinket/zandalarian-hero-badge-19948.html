Zandalarian Hero Badge
Binds when picked up
Unique
Trinket
Use: Increases your armor by 2000 and defense skill by 30 for 20 seconds. Every time you take melee or ranged damage, this bonus is reduced by 200 armor and 3 defense.
Added in patch 1.7
